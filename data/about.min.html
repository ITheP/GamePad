This project started as your typical small scale I know, i'll try and make my guitar hero controller wireless... that soon grew to displays, dynamic lighting, wifi, embedded web server, etc.etc.etc. blah blah blah.

And background shaders in the web pages.

Because.... reasons :)

The actual end goal is a decently re-usable re-targettable general purpose controller firmware that can, with a bit of electronic jiggery-pokery, be used for most controllers you could want to make or adapt.

Will have to see where this goes...
